IITK COVID Student Survey

A survey to study the impact of students traveling back home under high risk conditions,
and the impact on their families and communities.

Overview

The survey will be conducted in two parts and participation in both parts is vital.

This survey is intended for those students who were residing on campus and were instructed to vacate the campus by 26th April, 2021. If you were not on campus at this time, please skip this survey.
The first part is to be filled one week after leaving campus, and the second two weeks after leaving campus. We want to understand the difficulties faced by students of IITK during this period when India is combating the second wave of COVID.

About

The survey is being conducted by students. We want to understand the impact of the second wave of COVID on the health and well-being of the student community. We also hope that this effort helps make better policy decisions in the future.

Privacy

Since the health data is of very private nature, privacy is a major concern and the data will only be made public after anonymizing individual entries. No personally identifiable information will be put out as a result of this survey.
